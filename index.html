Common roofing questions.

These answers can help customers decide what to send first and understand how estimate requests are handled.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Customers can request a free estimate by phone or through the form. Final pricing depends on roof access, condition, materials, urgency, and the exact scope of work.

Do you work with insurance?

For storm damage or leak-related work, the team can document visible roof issues with photos and explain repair options. Insurance handling details should be confirmed directly before the job starts.

How fast can you come out?

Availability depends on schedule, location, weather, and urgency. Active leaks and emergency roof issues should be handled by calling directly for the fastest response.

Do I need to be home for an estimate?

Not always. For some exterior roof issues, photos, location, access notes, and a phone call may be enough to start. The team can confirm whether an on-site visit is needed.

Can I send roof photos first?

Yes. Photos are helpful for leaks, missing shingles, moss, gutters, flat roof membrane issues, and storm damage. Include wide shots, close-up details, and the city or ZIP if possible.

Ready to protect your roof?

Send a few roof photos, describe the issue, and Octopus Roofing Co LLC will help you understand the next step.
