Accessibility Statement

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Octopus Roofing Co LLC is committed to making its website accessible and usable for as many visitors as reasonably possible.

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide a website experience that is clear, readable, mobile-friendly, and usable with common assistive technologies. We aim to consider accessibility practices such as clear page structure, readable text, sufficient color contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, form labels, helpful error messages, alternative text for meaningful images, descriptive links and buttons, and mobile-friendly layouts.

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. As the website changes, we will continue working to identify and improve accessibility issues where reasonably possible.

Feedback

If you have difficulty accessing any part of the website, please contact us and describe the issue. We will make reasonable efforts to provide the information or service through an alternative method.
